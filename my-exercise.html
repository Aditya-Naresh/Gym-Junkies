Pull up

  1. 1. Grab the pull-up bar with your hands shoulder-width apart or wider.
  2. 2. Engage your back and core muscles by pulling your shoulder blades down and back.
  3. 3. Pull yourself up towards the bar, keeping your elbows close to your body.
  4. 4. Lower yourself back down to the starting position, focusing on controlled movement.
