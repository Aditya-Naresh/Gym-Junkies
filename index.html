Lunges

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your hands on your hips.
  2. Take a big step forward with your right foot, lowering your body until your right thigh is parallel to the floor.
  3. Keep your left foot in place and your left knee hovering just above the ground.
  4. Push back up to the starting position and repeat on the other side, stepping forward with your left foot.

Push up

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart and your feet together.
  2. Keep your core engaged and your back straight, and lower your body until your chest touches the ground.
  3. Push yourself back up to the starting position by straightening your arms and engaging your chest, shoulders, and triceps.
  4. Repeat for your desired number of reps.
